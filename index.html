About Me

Hello! My name is Jonathan Tayts and I am excited to dive into programming over the next few months. Over the course of my life I have developed many hobbies ranging from tennis to chess. One hobby I take passion in on a daily basis is volunteering for the Highland Park First Aid Squad. Whenever someone in Highland Park needs an ambulance we are dispatched. I get to work closely with neighboring hospital systems, first responders, and people that are calling at their worst times. The twelve hour shifts helped build character, and the good feeling of doing what is right for no reward helped develop me into a strong individual. One that is kind, caring, compassionate, but most of all an advocate for improvements within the healthcare system. I currently reside in Highland Park, NJ.
